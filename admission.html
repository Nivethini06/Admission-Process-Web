STEPS TO BE FOLLOWED

1.Create a student profile through login tab(Not necessary)

2.Click the admission tab to proceed further steps

3.Select your year

4.Fill the admission form correctly

5.Upload the necessary documents mentioned

6.Choose payment option to pay your tuition fee

7.Print your paid receipt

DOCUMENTS NEEDED

1.Allotment Order(Original)

2.Transfer Certificate and Conduct Certificate(Original)

3.10th Mark sheet(Original)

4.12th MArk sheet(Original)

5.Community Certificate(Original)

6.First graduate Certificate(Original)

7.Medical Fitness Certificate(Original)

8.Aadhar Certificate(Original)

9.Birth Certificate(Original)

10.Nativity Certificate(for students who haven't studied in TamilNadu for last 5 years)

11.Recent Income Certificate(Original)

12.Phyically Disable Certificate(If Applicable)

13.Bank Pass book Front Page

14.Address Proof(Original)
